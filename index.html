Aims and Objectives

Aims

1. To prepare nurses who can make independent decisions in nursing situations.

2. Protect the right of patients and facilitate individual & community participate and conduct research studies in areas of nursing.

3. To maintain a centre of excellence in nursing education with a view to generate new perspective & innovative approaches in scientific health care system.


Objectives

1. To promote and strengthen the nursing skill for better competence in the nursing profession in general and patient care in particular.
2. To meet the shortage of qualified nursing personnel in the country in general.
3. To enable them to get job opportunities for economically better position at national & international level.
4. To demonstrate leadership and managerial skill in clinical/community health setting.
5. To utilize the latest technology in providing health care by conducting nursing research, interpreting and utilizing the findings from health related research.

Historical Background

Historical Background


Sylhet a land hallowed by the memories of great saint Hazrat Shahjalal Yemeni (R), has been full of social, cultural and educational tradition from very early times. But in course of time these traditions are fading away. The literacy rate was falling dreadfully and mass people were deprived of adequate health care facilities. People from all walks of life including educationists, professionals and elite of the society were realizing the need of further development in health care delivery in this region.

In this perspective a group of doctors with the aim and objective of providing skilled medical manpower and quality health care service formed SMNC Private Ltd.
